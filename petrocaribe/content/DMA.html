A cable published by Wikileaks shows the “humorous” logistical issues Petrocaribe faced in Dominica.

Two incidents stand out, one where the captain of a ship delivering fuel was unhappy with the docking site and simply returned home, and another where the captain cut the pump halfway through a fuel delivery because he was pressed for time.

Venezuelan plans to build a refinery in Dominica drew widespread criticism from civil society and environmental groups, who feared damaging the country's "Nature Isle" image. The plans were eventually scrapped.

As the poorest country in the eastern Caribbean, Dominica was the first to join Venezuela's ALBA initiative, opening the door for St. Vincent and the Grenadines and Antigua and Barbuda to follow suit soon after.
